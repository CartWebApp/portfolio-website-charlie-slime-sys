MAYDAY

Completed: March 13th, 2025

All the way back in 2019, I drew a photo of a cat falling into the water digitally. It was inspired by the song MAYDAY by TheFatRat, taken by the idea of endlessly drifting into the deep blue seas. Now in 2025, I’ve drawn it to display how much I’ve actually improved over the years. This project helped further my creative process, understanding what it was like in the past, and developing it into something I can use for the future.
