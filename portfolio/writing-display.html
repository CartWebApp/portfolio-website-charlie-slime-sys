Mishap In The Hall

Read the document

This particular writing project is a display of my current writing skills. I collaborated with a project partner to get this story up and running. I wanted to figure out how to write out characters that have no particular build to them, only in personality. This means, I didn’t dive into any background of the character, nor did I bother having too much detail in their reactions. It was experimental by writing as if it were in an actual book.
