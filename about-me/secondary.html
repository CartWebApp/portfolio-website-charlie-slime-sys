My Future Plans

My plan after college is to become an art teacher, as I am passionate about sharing my love for art with others and inspiring the next generations of creativity after me. However, I also recognize the importance of financial stability.

To ensure I can support myself and my household, I intend to major in music as well as education and general arts. This will allow me to take on gig work on the side, providing an additional source of income.

As both an artist and a musician, I have the opportunity to accept commissions and requests. This not only gives my art portfolio more content but also adds to my earnings, depending on how frequently I receive commissions. By pursuing music alongside my teaching endeavors, I can safely sleep on the fear of becoming a "starving artist" and build a more secure future for myself.

Balancing these roles will enable me to thrive both personally and professionally while doing what I love the most in my life.
